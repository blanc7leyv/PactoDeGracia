Pacto de Gracia is a church called by God to expand the message of salvation of Jesus Christ to every nation. It is a place where one is able to grow spiritually and in close communion with their Creator. The Pastors of this congregation are Hector Martin Chavez and Maria Teresa Chavez. Pacto de Gracia is a community with a strong conviction of Jesus Christ. We are called to live a life in holiness and believe in the manifestations of the Holy Spirit. It is our mission to carry the message of Salvation.

Who is Jesus Christ?

Many people have more or less a concept of who Jesus is or what he has done. He is known by some as a prophet, others recognize Him as a healer, and and others consider him as a historical figure.
